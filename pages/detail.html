About this experience

Escape to the untouched paradise of Nusa Penida on this all-inclusive day trip from Bali. Discover the island's most iconic landmarks, including the famous Kelingking Beach with its T-Rex shaped cliff, the crystal-clear waters of Angel's Billabong, and the stunning natural arch of Broken Beach.

Your adventure begins with hotel pickup in South Bali, followed by a scenic 30-minute speedboat ride across the Badung Strait. Once on the island, explore breathtaking viewpoints, swim in natural infinity pools, and capture Instagram-worthy photos at every stop.
